About

Clint Eastwood is an American actor, director, and producer born in 1930. He became famous through Western films and later became one of the most respected directors in Hollywood.

Famous Movies

  • The Good, the Bad and the Ugly
  • Dirty Harry
  • Unforgiven
  • Million Dollar Baby

Awards

Eastwood has won multiple Academy Awards, including Best Director and Best Picture.
